About the Project

Inspiration

My inspiration for this project was to provide an alternative to assisted living, utilising emerging technologies, such as those present within Android Wear devices.

By using the sensors present within Android Wear devices, I am hoping to capture the majority of accidents and events that occur, when people are alone.

By creating such a solution, I aim to provide peace of mind to family members, carers and older adults, knowing that they are safe and within distance, no matter where they are.
